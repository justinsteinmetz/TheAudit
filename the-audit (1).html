The Bucket List

Everything you want to do before you—

“to kick
the bucket”

The phrase is older than the list. The list is a response to a problem: time runs out.

Named things
happen.

People who write a goal down are significantly more likely to do it. A list turns vague wanting into something you can act on.

But whose list
did you write?

The Audit

Skydiving. The Northern Lights. Santorini. Some wishes are yours. Some you absorbed. In the next ten minutes we find out which is which.

The Postcard Test

No judgement — just tag them.
Then ask: did I want this before I saw it somewhere?
